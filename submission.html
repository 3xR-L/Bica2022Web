2022 Annual International Conference on Brain-Inspired Cognitive Architectures for Artificial Intelligence

The 13th Annual Meeting of the BICA Society (BICA*AI 2022), Guadalajara, Mexico, hold on September 22-25.

Submission Categories

We welcome all forms of participation, with or without a paper.

  • Talk without a paper. Prepare only an abstract of approximately 150 words.

  • Short Procedia paper of 3-5 pages.

  • Regular Procedia paper of 6-8 pages. Extra pages might be possible, but will require the payment of additional Extra Page Fees. Any Procedia paper must be based on the provided template and cannot be shorter than 3 or longer than 10 pages.

  • Extended Cognitive Systems Research paper. The article size in this modality of submission is not limited to 8 pages, and there are no fees for extra pages. However, we recommend not exceeding more than 25 pages.

    • Submission Instructions

  • All papers should be formatted using the Procedia Computer Science template found here for their initial submission. The final publication venue for each submission will be determined by the Program Committee based on the paper quality, Authors’ preference and Reviewers’ recommendations.

  • Each paper submission must include at least 3 files: PDF, source (Word or Latex), and the signed Procedia Declaration form (also found at the above link).

  • Preparing the source file in MS Word: select "Save as —> Word Document (.docx)", then click "Remove Macros and Save". Upload unzipped.

  • Preparing the source file in Latex: zip the entire directory into one archive.

    Multiple submissions are encouraged; however, each accepted paper must be covered by its own Registration payment. We are looking forward to your submissions!
